A song. A melody of Aʹsaph.

O God, do not keep silent;

Do not be speechless or stay quiet, O God.

For look! your enemies are in an uproar;

Those who hate you act arrogantly.

With cunning they secretly plot against your people;

They conspire against your treasured ones.

They say: "Come, let us annihilate them as a nation,

So that the name of Israel may be remembered no more."

With one heart they plot together;

They form an alliance against you:

The tents of Eʹdom and the Ishʹma·el·ites,

Moʹab and the Haʹgar·ites,

Geʹbal, Amʹmon, and Amʹa·lek,

Phi·lisʹti·a and the inhabitants of Tyre.

Even As·syrʹi·a has joined them;

They support the sons of Lot. Seʹlah.

Do to them as you did to Midʹi·an,

As you did to Sisʹe·ra and to Jaʹbin at the stream of Kiʹshon.

They were annihilated at En-doʹr;

They became manure for the ground.

Make their nobles like Oʹreb and Zeʹeb,

And all their princes like Zeʹbah and Zal·munʹna,

Who said: "Let us take possession of

The land of God."

O my God, make them like thistledown,

Like stubble before the wind.

As a fire consumes a forest

And a flame sets mountains ablaze,

Chase after them with your windstorm,

And terrify them with your storm wind.

Fill their faces with disgrace,

So that they may search for your name, O Jehovah.

Let them be ashamed and terrified forever;

Let them die in disgrace,

So that people may know that you, whose name is Jehovah,

You alone are the Most High over all the earth.
